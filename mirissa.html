Secret Beach,Mirissa

Secret beach Mirissa is despite the name suggests, not really a secret anymore…It is a small pristine secluded beach and somewhat harder to get to. Hence the name: Secret Beach But it still is a beautiful V shaped beach with 2 bays on both sides to relax for a day. There isn’t much to do except some snorkeling and, of course, sunbathing. It’s not super easy or straight forward to get here, a little walk or really good motorbike skills are necessary to get to the entrance of the beach. Read in this blog post everything you need to know about Secret Beach Mirissa.

Coconut tree spot

Coconut Tree Hill is a unique cliff situated in Mirissa. This cliff is located at the end of a beach which is covered in coconut trees. Coconut Tree Hill is a popular tourist attraction in Mirissa and tourists flock here to capture beautiful images of this unique location. The cliff is located 4 m above sea level and is a famous location due to its picturesque beauty. The best time to visit Coconut Tree Hill would be in the morning… and by morning, we mean sunrise, about 5.30am

Parrot Rock

Parrot Rock in Mirissa is a stunning location that tourists can visit in a short amount of time. It is a rock formation next to the beach and surrounded by the ocean on three sides. Parrot Rock is just about 50 feet from the Mirissa Beach shore and visitors have to walk through the water and tourists can climb on top of the rock and witness a gorgeous panoramic view of the whole Mirissa Beach. You can come early in the morning to enjoy with the low tides and the amazing morning sea breeze.

Mirissa Beach

Mirissa is the land of beaches. Mirissa beach affords a picturesque view of the sunset and you can bask in the sun and watch as the chilled breeze gushes by your side. The beach is usually filled with surfers in the mornings and makes a good place for beginners to start surfing, as well. Once you are done with admiring the beach and the ocean, head to one of the beach bars to refuel yourselves. Mirissa does not have a very buzzing nightlife so you would have all the time in the world to relax.
