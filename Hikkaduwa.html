Hikkaduwa

Hikkaduwa is a seaside resort town in southwestern Sri Lanka.It’s located approximately 100km south of Colombo in the Galle District . Hikkaduwa is known for its strong surf and beaches,which has corals and home to marine turtles and exotic fish. Inland,Gangarama Maha Vihara is a Buddhist temple decorated with hand-painted murals. The town is considered one of the top tourist sites in Sri Lanka for both locals and international tourists.

Hikkaduwa Beach

Hikkaduwa Beach is reputed as the second best surfing spot in Sri Lanka. The best time of year to visit Hikkaduwa Beach is between November and March for the hot summer and good surf. Even though this is the peak season for tourists,Explore the white sand beaches and crystal blue waters of one of the best Sri Lanka Beaches,There are numerous swim sports and it has huge turtles swimming around close to shore which is why it is one of the best places to visit in Hikkaduwa.

Turtle Beach

There is one particular spot that always we can see turtles in Hikkaduwa. The spot is located in front of the Hikka Tranz Cinnamon hotel. You can easily get here from the main beach in Hikkaduwa, just turn left (if you’re facing the ocean) and walk up around the bay until you reach the end. Here, you will see a couple of signs about the turtles. This is where you are most likely to spot them!

Hikkaduwa Corals

Hikkaduwa is home to some of the best coral gardens in Asia.The most commonly found coral type here is Gampara, which are found in various forms, shapes and colours. Get a short boat ride over to the Hikkaduwa coral reef to get a closer look at the underwater world. There are many tour guides on Hikkaduwa beach who can help you to organise a Hikkaduwa snorkeling trip.It has many varieties of multi-coloured corals.
